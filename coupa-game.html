Builds on Week 1 — contract template knowledge required
Building on Week 1: You already know which contract template to use. This week we go deeper — once you have the right template, the Coupa request must be filled in correctly so it can progress through the approval workflow without errors or delays.

The 6 Mandatory Fields

Every Coupa request must have these fields completed correctly. A single error can block the request from reaching approvers.

Justification
Clear, jargon-free, understandable by an auditor with no business context.
Request Type
New, Renewal, Termination, Remediation, or RFX. Must accurately reflect the engagement.
Start and End Date
Must exactly match the dates in the signed contract.
Dollar Amount
Must match the contract value exactly. Incorrect amounts block financial approvals.
Legal Framework
GSA, HSA, CSA, or Low Risk Addendum. Must match the master agreement in place.
Type of Agreement
Amendment, Order Form, or Statement of Work. Describes the document being executed.
